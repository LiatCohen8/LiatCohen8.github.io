Wave

Liat Cohen developed the idea for and designed "Wave" magazine in 2015. She used Adobe Photoshop and Indesign to lay out each spread. Cohen also created a fully interactive and online-friendly PDF of her magazine as well.

The issue is an "Elle" inspired beach magazine with sections including health, fitness, fashion, trends, decor, and more. Cohen received outstanding feedback on this project.

All concepts and layouts are her design. She does NOT own the rights to any content or photos used throughout the magazine.
