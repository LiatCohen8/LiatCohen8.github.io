Video Production

High School. Unlabeled.

This short documentary is meant to show "the truth and nothing but the truth. High School. uncovered. uncensored. unlabeled." It was directed, produced, edited, and created by Liat Cohen in 2014 at her high school, Marin School of the Arts in Novato, California.

In June of Cohen's senior year of high school, she won first place in the Marin Teen Now Video Contest hosted at Oji Life Lab in San Rafael, California. The film was also showcased on the Marin School of the Arts website as a source of insight on life as a student at MSA for prospective and current students.
